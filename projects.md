# Research Projects

Current research and development efforts focus on large-scale data systems, practical blockchain infrastructure, and high-performance computing environments.

## Current Projects

### Big Blockchain Storage

- Korean title: 다양한 산업 분야 활용성 증대를 위한 대규모/대용량 블록체인 데이터 고확장성 분산 저장 기술 개발
- English summary: Development of highly scalable distributed storage technology for big blockchain data to expand adoption across diverse industries.
- Project site: [bbs-project.github.io](https://bbs-project.github.io/)

This project explores how blockchain data can be stored and managed more efficiently at scale, with emphasis on resilience, extensibility, and practical deployment.
