## About

Myungcheol Lee is a Principal Researcher at ETRI (Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute). His work centers on designing efficient and scalable systems for data-intensive environments, with particular interest in distributed computing, blockchain storage, cloud infrastructure, and artificial intelligence.

He has extensive experience in system engineering and large-scale data platforms, with a research focus on practical architectures that improve performance, reliability, and real-world usability.
